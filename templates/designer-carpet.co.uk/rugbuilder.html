What is Stainguard?

Stain Guard protection is beneficial for all carpets and floorcoverings. The protection solution acts by coating every fibre with a polymer shield which helps to resist the absorption of stains and dirt keeping your floor looking good for longer. However, the breathable properties of the carpet’s fibre are not affected.

What's in a cleaning kit?

The Crucial Trading wool safe stain remover and cleaning set contains;
-Shampoo (500ml) for large area cleaning and general stain removal.
-Stain remover (250ml) which gently and effectively removes a wide range of stains such as fat, fruit juices cola, coffee (up to 90%), red wine, tea, ketchup, grass, food, soot, rust, chewing gum, marker pens and many other stains.
-Lint-free cloth.
-Natural fibre brush.

What is Anti-Slip Matting?

Anti-slip matting is attached to the back of the rug to prevent it from moving around on hard floor surfaces
