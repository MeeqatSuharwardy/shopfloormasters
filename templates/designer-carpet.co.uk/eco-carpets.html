Truly Sustainable

Every carpet in the Eco Collection is crafted from 100% natural, undyed wool with no added dyes or blocking agents. The individual colours are created by carefully blending various types of wools from renewable sources, and the carpets are weaved by hand, on traditional handlooms, using natural jute and cotton - materials that are again derived from renewable resources. The final finishing process uses latex straight from the rubber tree, ensuring a truly sustainable floor covering.

Completely Biodegradable

Did you know that the majority of carpets produced today are made using nylon, polypropylene or polyester fibres? All of these materials are derived from oil, meaning carpets that include them will typically take up to 500 years to break down in the soil. Not so with the Eco Carpet collection. When it comes to disposing of our carpets at the end of their useful life cycle, they can either be recycled or will naturally biodegrade in a period of just 1-5 years without adding any harmful chemicals back into the earth.
